## What the video shows

The demo video walks through the following features of the portfolio website:

1. **Navigation** – sticky navbar, smooth scroll between sections
2. **Responsive design** – resizing the browser window to show mobile layout
3. **Mobile menu** – hamburger button opening the nav on a narrow screen
4. **CSS Grid cards** – the projects section layout
5. **Image slideshow** – navigating between slides manually and auto-play
6. **FAQ accordion** – opening and closing accordion items
7. **Contact form** – filling in the form and seeing the success message
8. **GitHub Pages** – the live deployed URL shown in the browser
